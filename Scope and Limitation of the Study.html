The study also have a scope and its limitations to deal with. The scope and limitations will be discussed separately.

Scope

  • The system can manage the information of the students and teachers.
  • The system can back-up and restore database, in case of database lose.
  • The system also generates reports from enrollment details, list of student, class program, total number of enrolleesand total number of students in every teacher.
  • Printing of study load is included.
  • The system can also add subjects for every grade/level.
  • The system can create schedule for the teacher.
  • The system can add and update the student and the teacher information.

Limitation of the Study

This study has its own limitations and that is; the system is only to register and enroll students who will be enrolled in the institutions. It does not cover the account transaction, and asking for grades.

  • The system focuses only on registration and enrollment of the student.
  • The computation of necessary fees are not included in this study even the handling of grades to the student.
  • Preparing of reports are not included.
