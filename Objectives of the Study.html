The overall objective of the study is to improve current manual enrollment system. The study aims to:

  • Analyze user and system requirements related to the enrollment process and services
  • Develop a system that will provide efficient and centralized management of enrollment information system.
  • Develop a system that will provide enrollment information availability, security and timely enrollment reporting capabilities
  • Relevant enrollment system
  • Promote consistency, reliable and up-to-date enrollment information
