The Computerized Enrollment System will be beneficial MCC. The proposed system will provide improvement on the current manual enrollment process of the school. Specifically, the system will also be beneficial to the following:

Administration - The main beneficiary of the study is Mandaue City College that will be adopting the innovation of technology through computers that will make the work with optimum performance.

Registrar - Concerning the registrar`s office. The proposed system will help them in maintaining the data students and the institutions will not use the manual data gathering. The staff needs to check the database for the records needed.

Student - The proposed system will able to minimize the time and effort that it takes to process student`s enrollment. It is beneficial also because it will reduce the time to be consumed by the student`s during enrollment.

Researchers - This study will equip them with knowledge about the enrollment system.

Future Researchers - This study may serve as basis of future research related to Computerized Enrollment System.
